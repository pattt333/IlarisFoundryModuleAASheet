## Why

The AI creature generator currently produces only weapons and optional vorteile. Creatures in Ilaris also have Eigenschaften — descriptive properties and capabilities like "Astralsinn", "Flieger", "Regeneration III", "Schreckgestalt II" that define their special rules. These are critical for creature statblocks and should be generated by the AI alongside weapons.

## What Changes

- Add an `EIGENSCHAFTEN` list (~20 entries with one-line German descriptions) to the AI prompt (~500 tokens)
- Add `"eigenschaften": ["Name"]` to the JSON schema in the prompt
- Add eigenschaften validation: clamp against known list, drop invalid ones
- Add eigenschaften cache (like vorteile) or hardcode the list
- Create embedded `eigenschaft` items with names and descriptions on the generated actor

## Capabilities

### Modified Capabilities

- `ai-creature-generation`: The prompt SHALL include a categorized eigenschaften list with descriptions. Validation SHALL check eigenschaften against the known list. Generated actors SHALL receive embedded eigenschaft items with their German descriptions.

## Impact

- **Modified files**: `scripts/utilities.js` (prompt, validator), `scripts/apps/ai-creature-dialog.js` (item creation)
- **No new files**
- **No Hook changes**
